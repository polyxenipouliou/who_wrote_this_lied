## Reference Writing Notes

### IEEE Format Requirements:
- Authors: Initials followed by surname
- Article titles: Sentence case, in quotation marks
- Journal/Conference titles: Title case, italicized
- Volume/issue: vol. X, no. Y
- Page numbers: pp. XXX–YYY
- DOI: Include when available
- URLs: Include for online resources with access date
